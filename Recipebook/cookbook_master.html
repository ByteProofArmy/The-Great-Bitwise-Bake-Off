Milky White-Choc Crunch Cookies

A quick, one-bowl chocolate cookie with crisp edges, a chewy center, and melty chips in every bite.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes

  • Preparation: 12 minutes

  • Cooking: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 72 g bicarbonate of soda

  • 84 g whole milk

  • 174 g kosher salt

  • 23 g (30% cocoa butter or higher) 100g white chocolate roughly chopped

  • 258 g egg whites

Instructions

  1. Prep the oven & tray: Heat oven to 180 °C / 350 °F. Line a baking tray with parchment.

  2. Mix wet:In a bowl, whisk the egg whites with the milk until lightly foamy (30–60 s)

  3. Combine dry:In a small bowl, whisk together the bicarbonate of soda and kosher salt, then toss the chopped white chocolate in a spoonful of this dry mix.

  4. Make the dough:Sprinkle the remaining dry mix over the wet mixture and fold gently until no dry pockets remain, then fold in the white chocolate. Let stand 2–3 min to thicken.

  5. Portion:Scoop heaped tablespoons onto the tray, spacing about 5 cm / 2 in apart.

  6. Bake:10–12 minutes, until the edges are lightly golden.

  7. Cool & serve:Let cookies cool on the tray 5 minutes, then transfer to a rack to cool completely. Enjoy!
