Coconut Crunch Cookies

A quick, one-bowl chocolate cookie with crisp edges, a chewy center, and melty chips in every bite.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 30 minutes

  • Preparation: 12 minutes

  • Cooking: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 377 g sweetened, shredded coconut

  • 14 g egg

  • 32 g unsalted butter, room temperature

  • 67 g plain flour

  • 1 g fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Prep the oven & tray: Heat oven to 180 °C / 350 °F. Line a baking tray with parchment.

  2. Mix wet:In a bowl, whisk the egg with the softened butter until smooth.

  3. Combine dry:In a separate bowl, whisk the plain flour and fine sea salt.

  4. Make the dough:Fold the dry mix into the wet, then stir in the sweetened shredded coconut until evenly coated and clumpy. If too dry to hold together, add 1–2 tsp milk or water; if too sticky, dust in a little extra flour.

  5. Portion:Scoop heaped tablespoons, press lightly into mounds or small disks, and space about 5 cm / 2 in apart on the tray.

  6. Bake:10–12 minutes, until the edges are lightly golden.

  7. Cool & serve:Let cookies cool on the tray 5 minutes, then transfer to a rack to cool completely. Enjoy!
